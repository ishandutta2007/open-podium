# OpenPodium: The Open-Source, AI-Powered Lead Management Platform

**OpenPodium** is a next-generation, open-source platform designed to unify the best of lead management, customer communication, and business automation into a single, powerful, and customizable solution. Inspired by industry leaders like Podium, Thryv, Birdeye, GoHighLevel, Odoo, and HubSpot, OpenPodium is built from the ground up for the way the world works with AI today.

## About The Project

In today's competitive landscape, businesses are forced to juggle multiple tools for lead capture, communication, marketing, and analytics. This fragmentation leads to data silos, inefficiencies, and a disjointed customer experience.

OpenPodium aims to solve this by providing a unified, AI-driven platform that incorporates the most powerful features from leading solutions:

- **The conversational power of Podium:** Engage with leads and customers seamlessly across multiple channels.
- **The all-in-one approach of Thryv and Birdeye:** Manage your online reputation, reviews, and customer interactions in one place.
- **The marketing automation of GoHighLevel and HubSpot:** Nurture leads, automate campaigns, and track your entire sales funnel.
- **The extensibility of Odoo:** Customize and extend the platform to fit your unique business needs.

Our vision is to create a flexible, powerful, and affordable solution that empowers businesses of all sizes to grow and thrive in the AI era.

## Key Features

OpenPodium is being built with a comprehensive feature set to manage the entire customer lifecycle:

- **Unified Inbox:** Consolidate communications from SMS, email, social media, and web chat into a single, AI-powered inbox.
- **AI-Powered Lead Nurturing:** Automatically qualify, score, and nurture leads with intelligent, personalized communication.
- **Review & Reputation Management:** Monitor and respond to reviews from dozens of sites, and automatically request new ones.
- **Automated Marketing Campaigns:** Build sophisticated marketing funnels with email, SMS, and social media automation.
- **CRM & Contact Management:** Get a 360-degree view of your customers with a fully-featured, integrated CRM.
- **Appointment Scheduling:** A smart, self-service scheduling tool that syncs with your calendars.
- **Payments & Invoicing:** Send invoices and collect payments directly through the platform.
- **Analytics & Reporting:** Gain deep insights into your business with customizable dashboards and AI-driven reports.
- **Extensible & Open-Source:** Built on a modern, modular architecture that allows for easy customization and extension.
